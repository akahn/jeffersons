The Jeffersons: Visualizing Intergenerational Income Mobility

This visualization shows income levels by quintile with the cream colored dots at the top representing the richest people in society and the blue dots at hte bottom representing the poorest people. Given a certain level* of income mobility for each quintile, we observe how society evolves over time.

* You have a 25% chance moving up or down one level in society unless you're already at the top or bottom.
